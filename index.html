Unifying Service & Vision

Leo District 306 D1 has been a beacon of service, creating community solutions and training young leaders for decades.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in community and humanitarian service. We envision a world where every community has a dedicated team of volunteers working together to restore sight, conserve the ecosystem, combat hunger, and empower youth to lead the future.

Our Mission

To empower volunteers to serve their communities, meet humanitarian needs, encourage peace and promote international understanding through Lions and Leo Clubs. We provide critical operational frameworks, training, and platforms for sustainable impact.

Our Operational Pillars

Leadership

Cultivating active leadership through structured youth development and cabinet roles.

Global Reach

Deploying localized community relief campaigns that conform to global targets.

Fellowship

Building lifelong professional and civic connections between Lions and Leos.

Integrity

Maintaining complete transparency and strict audit trails on all service investments.

2005

District Inauguration

Leo District 306 D1 was formally chartered by Lions Clubs International, starting with 12 charter clubs and 250 members.

2012

Youth Leo Expansion

The youth Leo Club wing was created, bringing on board 15 school and community Leo clubs to cultivate junior leadership.

2018

District Eye Clinic Foundation

Purchased and deployed the first dedicated mobile vision screening unit, providing free screening to rural schools.

2025

Carbon-Zero Milestone

Completed the historic 'Green Canopy' drive, planting and tracking 50,000 trees across regional deforestation zones.
